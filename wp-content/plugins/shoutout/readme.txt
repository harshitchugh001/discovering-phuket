== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**What is ShoutOut?**
ShoutOut is a multi level marketing solution and cost effective alternative to Google, Facebook or Instagram Ads. It provides a mechanism to administer an unlimited number of affiliate and influencer referrals, and provide them with the tools to promote your products and drive traffic and sales to your online ecommerce store.

**What is the cost of ShoutOut?**
It&#39;s just $59 a month. Far less than the cost of even one day&#39;s traditional advertising!
You get access to the software that allows you to create and track an UNLIMITED number of influencers and affiliates. You can try the WooCommerce Affiliate Plugin with no restrictions or limitations for a full 7 days totally for FREE.

**Is there a minimum contract term?**
No, simply pay each month, cancel anytime you wish.

**Can I customize ShoutOut with my own logo and branding?**
Yes. Each affiliate/influencer will have access to their very own dashboard/control center and it may be branded with your own logo and matching colors. If you include your logo, we&#39;ll also ensure every email has it included to make them look professional and being sent from you!

**Can I easily invite potential affiliates and influencers into my network?**

*How do they signup to join my referral program?*
ShoutOut creates a sign-up page that you can brand as your own. The software also includes a professionally written template and email engine that allows you to simply insert an email address into an invitation, and it will send an automated signup form to your potential affiliate/influencer. It is incredibly easy.

*Do you include tutorials?*
Yes, you&#39;ll gain full access to our comprehensive Affiliate Marketing Video Course covering over 29 subjects. Don&#39;t pay for an external course to learn affiliate marketing, our professional, step by step guide is included.

**How long does a referral link track a customer?**
You may set the length of time customers are tracked by ShoutOut (even if the customer doesn&#39;t use a referral link on subsequent visits). ShoutOut also supports tracking via coupon codes, QR codes, and can remember affiliate/customer associations (perfect for subscription-based businesses).

**Is my data secure?**
ShoutOut is trusted by some of the world&#39;s largest online merchants. Don’t risk building your valuable referral network with Apps that may not be around 6 months from now. Our servers are replicated across the globe to ensure data integrity, reliability, speed, and high performance. Your data is also fully encrypted and backed up hourly, and we combine all this with our highly responsive customer service.
